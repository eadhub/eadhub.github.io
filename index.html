》About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Stirling, focusing on the intersection of psychology, technology, and mental health. My research combines evidence-based therapeutic approaches with gamification principles to create an effective digital intervention for social anxiety.

With a background in both Graphic Design and Psychology, I bring a unique perspective to digital mental health solutions, emphasizing both user experience and therapeutic efficacy.
